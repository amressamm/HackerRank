      I worked at Code Ninja as an intern

      Pros

      - open pantry
      - free food fridays
      - no dress code
      - friendly co-workers and colleagues
      - challenging yet fulfilling tasks and projects
      - learned and acquired a lot of new technical skills and knowledge
      - good workplace layout and setup, which gives that "Google" workplace feel
      - workstation is not isolated like a normal office cubicle
      - more relaxed and cozy environment compared to a stereotypical office

      Cons

      - location: Eastwood, which is prone to traffic

      Advice to Management

      - formal and intensive technical trainings and workshops to improve technical skills and knowledge of workers
